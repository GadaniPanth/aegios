About Us

Aegios Polyfilms Private Limited is an emerging force in manufacturing sustainable BOPET films, Metalized, BOPP films, and Polyester (Resins & Chips) in India. Born under the Atmanirbhar India Mission, we combine advanced European technologies with a strong focus on recycling and green production. The company has set up a state-of-the-art greenfield project in Logate district, Kathua, Jammu & Kashmir, to manufacture BOPET & Polyester Chips. The 10.6-meter BOPET line is currently operational, with the Resin plant set to commence operations in March 2024. Additionally, we are on track to commission the 10.4-meter BOPP line by the end of 2025.
